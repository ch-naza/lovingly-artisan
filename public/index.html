Who We Are

Lovingly Artisan is an award-winning bakery based in Cumbria, committed to the craft of traditional bread making. Every loaf is made using slow fermentation techniques, quality local ingredients, and a genuine care for the people who eat it. We bake for daily life, for celebrations, for gifting and for anyone who believes good bread is worth taking seriously.

Sourdough on Subscription

Never be without good bread. With our weekly, fortnightly or monthly subscriptions, you can look forward to regular deliveries of your favourite loaves, pastries and bread boxes. A bread subscription makes a brilliant present for a friend or family member — especially young offspring who may be setting up home for the first time. Simply click the subscription option next to the bread or breads of your choice and let us know where and how often you'd like to receive them.
